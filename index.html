🥚 How to Play

🎯 The Goal

Crack as many eggs as you can, score the most points, and beat your personal best. Pick any egg in any order — but the grid is laid out to naturally ramp up the challenge!

🥚 The Eggs & Points

  • Row 1: 10pt · 10pt · 10pt · ⚡Risk · 10pt
  • Row 2: 20pt · 20pt · ⚡Risk · 25pt · 25pt
  • Row 3: 30pt · 30pt · 30pt · 30pt · ⚡Risk
  • Row 4: 50pt · 50pt · ⚡Risk · ⚡Risk · 50pt
  • Row 5: ✨100pt · ✨100pt (hidden — unlock after all 4 Risk Eggs)

📝 Attempts

  • Regular eggs: 2 attempts per question. Use your hint wisely!
  • ⚡ Risk eggs: 1 attempt only — no second chances!
  • ✨ Hidden eggs: 1 attempt only — these are the toughest!

⚡ Risk Eggs — The Gamble

  • Play It Safe: Answer correctly = +30 pts. Wrong = 0 pts, no penalty
  • Risk It All: Correct = DOUBLE your entire score. Wrong = LOSE everything
  • One shot only — choose wisely!

🏆 Bonuses

  • 🔥 Streak: 3 correct in a row = +10 bonus pts
  • ⚡ Speed: Answer within 10 seconds = +5 bonus pts
  • 🔮 Prophecy: Predict the last egg you'll crack = +50 pts if correct!

✅ Finishing

Hit Done any time to end your game. You'll see full stats and the correct answers to eggs you missed.
